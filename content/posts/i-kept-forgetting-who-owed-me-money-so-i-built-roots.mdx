i had a money problem. not earning.... tracking 🥲

i was spending a lot. sometimes lending to friends. and then just.... forgetting about it.

my system was a notepad app in my phone. every entry typed by hand. every total calculated by me, again and again.

who owes me. who i owe. what already came back. pure confusion 😮‍💨

<Pull>the notepad never forgot anything. but it never calculated anything either. that part was still me.</Pull>

so i built my own app and called it **roots**.

expenses, income, lending, borrowing.... all per person. repayments tracked. budgets that warn me before i cross them. recurring stuff handled automatically. works offline, installs on my phone like a normal app.

i made it stupid simple on purpose.

<Ex>Ex: lent ₹500 to a friend → one entry. he pays ₹200 back → one tap. the app remembers the ₹300. not me.</Ex>

then i gave it to my friends.

now they use it daily and they love it. people using something you built for yourself.... thats a different kind of feeling 🙏

the app → [Roots](https://expensetracker-seven-zeta.vercel.app)
