my father is a paint contractor.

every time he finishes painting a bank branch, the owner needs a bill.... material lists, work measurements, totals. all of it.

and every time, that bill was ME 🥲

he calls me, tells me the items, and i sit with ms-word. typing each line one by one. adding colors to fonts. adjusting sizes. then a calculator for every single total. then export pdf and send.

i did this for years.

and so many times i made calculation mistakes.... a wrong total on a real bill going to a real owner. my father got the complaint. i got the scolding 😮‍💨

<Pull>getting scolded by your father over a wrong total is a different kind of code review.</Pull>

so i built the fix properly.

an android app + a web app, both talking to the same database in real time. sections, work measurements, per-day rates, advances.... and a one-tap PDF where the maths is never wrong.

<Ex>Ex: a bill that used to eat my whole evening now takes minutes. and the totals are always right.</Ex>

honest part: my father still doesnt use the app himself. he still calls me 🤷‍♂️ but now i open the app while he talks, and the pdf is ready before the call ends.

i didnt build it for a resume. i built it because i was tired of being wrong.

turns out thats the best reason to build anything.

the app → [Site Bill Automator](https://web-sable-rho-20.vercel.app)
