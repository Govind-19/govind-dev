PR reviews at our place have this one recurring comment that used to annoy me....

"rename this."

handleClick to handleStudentRowClick. getData to fetchAttendanceSummary. on and on.

the code WORKED. the names were fine to me. i wrote them, i knew what they did 🤷‍♂️

it felt like bikeshedding. arguing about paint while the house is being built.

then six months passed.

i had to fix a bug in a feature i wrote myself. searched for the function.... couldnt find it. i had three different getData's in three files and no idea which one was mine.

<Pull>I was a stranger in my own code. Six months is all it took.</Pull>

<Ex>Ex: grep "getData" → 14 results. grep "fetchAttendanceSummary" → 1 result. thats the whole argument right there.</Ex>

that i when i understood. names arent for the day you write the code. theyre for the day you come back.

and someone always comes back. usually you. usually in a hurry.

now im the one leaving "rename this" comments 🥲
